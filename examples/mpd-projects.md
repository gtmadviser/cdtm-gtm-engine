# CDTM MPD — starter ICP ideas (cohort context)

High-level notes for the four MPD teams. These are **loose starting points** for picking a B2B cold-outbound ICP, not the answer. Sharpen yours with Claude using `prompts/icp.md`: a specific *who* + a *trigger* that says "now".

**If your product is consumer or hardware** (Meta, Poke): you don't cold-email end users. Pick the **business segment that looks like a buyer, a design partner, or a distribution channel**, and target that. Same engine, smarter audience.

---

## BMW — AI for blue-collar work
Building AI that fits production, workshop, maintenance and aftersales, capturing frontline feedback as a "data flywheel."

Starter ICPs for outbound (to find design partners / validate):
- **Ops, plant or maintenance managers** at mid-size manufacturers (roughly 50 to 500 staff, DACH) with shop-floor or field teams. Trigger: hiring for "Industrie 4.0" / digitalization / maintenance roles, or a recent MES or IoT rollout. Angle: capture technician know-how without adding a step to their day.
- **Heads of aftersales / service** at machinery or automotive suppliers. Trigger: a new product line or service expansion. Angle: turn service feedback into a learning loop.

## Meta (supervised by Felix Wolf) — assistive vision hardware
Hardware + computer vision that reintroduces lost visual field for people with permanent vision loss. Patient-facing, so target the orgs that serve or reach them:
- **Low-vision and ophthalmology clinics, optometrists, neuro-rehab centers** (DACH). Trigger: clinics advertising low-vision services or new equipment. Angle: a pilot device for patients with visual-field loss.
- **Assistive-tech distributors, rehab centers, associations for the blind and visually impaired.** Trigger: grants or funding for assistive technology. Angle: design partners + a route to patients.

## Phoros — payroll automation for tax advisors
B2B SaaS automating payroll (Lohnbuchhaltung) for German tax-advisory firms. The cleanest cold-outbound case:
- **Owners / managing partners of small to mid Steuerberater firms** (roughly 2 to 50 staff, Germany) that run payroll in-house. Trigger: hiring a Lohnbuchhalter, or job posts mentioning payroll workload. Angle: cut the manual payroll grind and keep the recurring revenue.
- **Dedicated payroll-service providers (Lohnbüros).** Trigger: growth or hiring. Angle: automate the highest-volume sub-processes.
- Tip: you already have design-partner firms. List what they share (size, software, region) and hunt lookalikes (see `prompts/icp.md`).

## Poke — viral AI assistants (B2C)
A consumer product on the Poke platform. You won't cold-email consumers, so target the people who look like ideal early users, or who give you distribution:
- **Tech-first, AI-native founders at sub-5-person startups** (the prosumers who adopt and amplify new AI tools). Trigger: just launched on Product Hunt, posting about AI tooling, active in indie-hacker communities. Angle: be an early power user, or co-build a "recipe".
- **Creators and community leaders in productivity or AI niches** (for partnerships and reach). Trigger: an active newsletter or Discord. Angle: a collaboration that puts you in front of their audience.

---

Remember: pick **one** sharp ICP + a trigger, source 25 to 50 real leads (`scripts/source_leads.py`), and write copy that could only be sent to that person. Small and real beats big and fake.
