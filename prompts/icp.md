# ICP definition

Goal: go from a vague market to a **sharp** ideal customer profile with a trigger. Sharper ICP makes every downstream step easier.

> **Fast way to sharpen:** list 3 best-fit and 3 worst-fit would-be customers. The gap between them *is* your ICP. No customers yet? Look at a competitor's most-loved customers instead.

## The framework — answer these

1. **Who, specifically?** Industry/vertical + company size + role of the person who feels the pain and can say yes. Not "SMBs" — "owner of a 5–20 person dental practice in DACH."
2. **What pain, in their words?** The problem as *they* would describe it, not your feature.
3. **The trigger — why now?** A signal that this account needs you *this week*. Pick one from this catalog (or find your own):

   | Trigger | How to detect it | Message angle |
   |---|---|---|
   | Hiring for a relevant role | job boards, careers page, LinkedIn Jobs | "Saw you're hiring a [role], usually means [pain] is on the table" |
   | New funding / expansion | news, Crunchbase, "we raised" posts | "Congrats on the raise, teams at this stage usually hit [problem]" |
   | Leadership change | LinkedIn, press | "New [title]s often re-evaluate [area] in their first 90 days" |
   | Uses a specific tool | BuiltWith, job posts, their site | "Noticed you run [tool], [your product] plugs the [gap]" |
   | Recent launch / update | their blog, socials, Product Hunt | "Saw you just launched X, that usually creates [need]" |
   | Recent review / complaint | Google/Trustpilot/G2, socials | "Saw reviews mention [issue], that's exactly what we fix" |
4. **Where do they congregate?** One channel you can actually reach them on.
5. **Disqualifiers.** Who looks like a fit but isn't (too big, wrong geo, no budget).

## Output (write to `output/icp.md`)

One tight paragraph, e.g.:

> We target **[who]** who **[pain]**. The trigger we hunt is **[signal]**, which we can detect via **[source]**. We reach them on **[channel]**. We skip **[disqualifier]**. Our north-star metric is **[metric]**.

Keep it to 4–6 sentences. If you can't name a trigger, the ICP isn't sharp enough yet.
